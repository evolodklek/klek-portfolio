Key Projects

Geo‑Biosphere Lab (2025)
C‑13 pulse‑chase · PLFA/PCA · tillage effects

Led C‑13 labeling on rendzic Luvisol under contrasting tillage. Quantified 4× higher belowground C allocation in ploughed vs. harrowed soils (ANOVA p<0.01); PLFA‑PCA showed strong Actinobacteria response (F=13.27, p=0.015). Demonstrates experience with EA‑IRMS, microbial biomarkers and isotope mixing models.

SLA Terrace Walls (2025)
Urban ecology · trait gradients · light variability

Measured specific leaf area (SLA) for 64 wall‑rooted plants (mostly Hedera helix) along light availability and variability gradients. Observed decreasing SLA with higher light intensity/variability and greater plasticity in shaded microsites.

Oil Remediation (2023–2024)
Field sampling · NDVI time‑series · bioremediation

Independently sampled crude‑oil‑contaminated sites (up to 8019 m², 0.2–2 m depth), interpreted lab TPH results (0.011–0.287 mg/kg) and adapted bioremediation plans. Designed SPOT/Landsat‑based NDVI monitoring showing vegetation drop from 0.77 to 0.69 between 1995 and 2023.

Marine Geology Workshop
Core description · sedimentology

Processed Indian Ocean sediment core: split and logged core, polished surfaces, described lithology and sedimentary structures, and performed microscopy.

HiWi Biogeology
Stable isotopes · collagen & enamel

Conducted collagen extraction from bone, sampled tooth enamel and applied amino‑acid protocols for stable isotope analyses.
